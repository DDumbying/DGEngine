## Intro to Game AI

Game AI is not really AI in the broad sense of the word.

- Talking specifically about gameplay AI (not nalytics, content creation, etc..)

The overall goal of Game AI is to facilitate gameplay.

This can be achieved in various ways based on theh game.

- Generally, we aim to provide some form of interaction for the player

So we need to mention also that the **Realism** is almost never the goal.

Also we don't need to make a Game AI seem like A Super complecated problem, because it's not.

And that there is a BIG miscommunication/misunderstanding of that we need to stop thinking that we are building actuall AI.

We are building Controllable reaction systems like.

in almost **All** cases, game AI is solved via *Rule-based Reaction Systems*, essentially an information management proglem.
Fundamentally ses a lot of concepts from **Descrete Mathemateics** like *Graph theory*, *Combiatorics*, *Propositional logic*, *fuzzy logic*, etc..
